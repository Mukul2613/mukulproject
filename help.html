Welcome to the HIT AND MISS GAME

This Game consists of 3 levels namely EASY LEVEL, MEDIUM, HARD LEVEL.
In this game, gifts are hidden at the random position and you need to guess the correct position
of gifts in minimum number of chances. As Soon as you clicks on the box,
there maybe three posibilities maybe you get a GIFT or MISS or maybe DANGER.

Make sure you did not get that "DANGER☠️" sign otherwise your whole progress
will be lost and your game will be restarted automatically.

So, ENJOY!!
